# Session Recap — 2026-04-16

**Project:** academic-works-harvest
**Session focus:** Harvest Hunter College LibGuides A-Z records via OAI-PMH, compare against prior harvest, convert to Excel, and explore `dc:source` field values across Academic Works oai_dc records.

## What we built / changed
- Harvested **208 records** from the Hunter College LibGuides A-Z endpoint (`libguides.library.hunter.cuny.edu/oai.php`) using OAI-PMH with resumption tokens. Saved as `data/hunter_libguides_az.xml`.
- Compared the new harvest against the **Sept 18, 2025 harvest** (515 records) — identified **307 records** that disappeared between harvests. All 307 were "active" records (not deleted), mostly database/journal package entries and reference tool links.
- Converted the Hunter LibGuides XML to an **Excel spreadsheet** (`data/hunter_libguides_az.xlsx`) for colleagues unfamiliar with XML. Styled with 12pt Arial per preference.
- Listed all **87 unique `dc:source` values** across the 133,195 oai_dc Academic Works records, sorted by count. These are essentially collection/series names from Academic Works (e.g., "Publications and Research" at 38,192 records, "Dissertations, Theses, and Capstone Projects" at 32,774).

## Key decisions
- **Store Hunter LibGuides harvest in this project** — **why:** it's the same workflow (OAI-PMH harvesting for Primo VE import) even though it's a different source system (Springshare vs. Digital Commons).
- **Plan to map `dc:source` → `dcterms:relation` in Primo VE normalization** — **why:** `dc:source` contains the Academic Works collection name, which is useful context for discovery but wasn't previously being harvested. `dcterms:relation` is an available target field in Primo VE normalization rules.

## Gotchas & things to remember
- **Hunter LibGuides OAI endpoint uses `oai.php`** (Springshare), not `do/oai/` like Academic Works (Digital Commons). URL format: `libguides.library.hunter.cuny.edu/oai.php?verb=ListRecords&metadataPrefix=oai_dc&set=az`.
- **Only 1 of 208 LibGuides records has `dc:subject`** — Springshare doesn't require subjects on LibGuides entries, so almost none have them. The lone exception: "17th & 18th Century Burney Collection" with subjects "English Language & Literature" and "Theatre".
- **307 records vanished between Sept 2025 and April 2026** — this wasn't a harvest error; the records genuinely disappeared from the endpoint. Worth flagging to Hunter if they didn't intentionally remove them.
- **`dc:source` in Academic Works has 87 distinct values** across 133K records, and every record has one. These are collection/series names, not journal-level sources.
